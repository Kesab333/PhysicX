Core Capabilities

Classical Mechanics

⚡

Electromagnetic Fields

Visualize electric and magnetic field lines, equipotential surfaces, Lorentz force vectors, and full Maxwell equation solutions in 2D and 3D field maps.

Wave & Optics Lab

Why PhysicX

not just developers

High-Performance ODE Solver

Adaptive Runge–Kutta 4/5 integrator with automatic step-size control ensures stability at interactive speeds. Handles stiff coupled systems without slowdown.

Physical Accuracy

IEEE 754 double-precision arithmetic throughout. All simulation results cross-validated against known analytical solutions for standard benchmark problems.
